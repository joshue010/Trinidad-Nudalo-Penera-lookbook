DEADWAYS

Deadways has some of the most exclusive apparel you would love to sport in your wardrobe. From cool to flashy designs, this store offers some of the most fashionable streetwear you want to step out in. Every design is unique and reflects an attitude that tops up comfort and style. With its extensive catalog, including everything from tees, bottoms, bags, hats, and accessories, the store has won the attention of hundreds of buyers in the Filipino market.

STRAP

Strap is a cebuano-estblished local brand that showcases the cebuano side of skateboarding. This brand includes homegrown talents that contributes to the filipino-pride, namely: Margielyn Didal and Motic Panugalinog. The brand features quality products such as footwear, skate and work pants, t-shirts and many more. They are located at Juana Osmena Extension, Cebu City.

NARTHATIC

Narthatic is a cebu based brand made for authentic skateboarding. They feature hand made products and are customizable to the taste of the customers. They also showcase skate clips which appeal to the taste of the mass.
